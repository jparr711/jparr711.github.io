Gregory Maly

About Me

Hello! I'm Gregory Maly, and I focus on integrating data science into efforts to address social and environmental challenges. I specialize in building practical, technology-based solutions that turn complex data into actionable insights, assembling and leading diverse teams with a range of perspectives, and helping organizations navigate the rapidly evolving technology landscape to achieve their goals. The following project examples are selected highlights from my work that I am able to share publicly.

This project is a proof of concept showcasing the use of machine learning to create structured datasets from unstructured news articles. It highlights how AI can extract meaningful insights from raw text, paving the way for more up-to-date information and smarter data-driven decision-making.

PHR Management and Analytics

In 2018, a close family member was diagnosed with a rare blood disease called Severe Aplastic Anemia. To help us track routine Combined Blood Cell (CBC) tests and medications, I built a Personal Health Records (PHR) management system.

Tools: R/Shiny, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Spa Creek Water Testing

Spa Creek Conservancy in Annapolis, MD conducts weekly water tests in over a dozen sites in Spa Creek and Back Creek. This platform provides a weekly update on results.

Tools: R/Shiny, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
DPG Explorer

The Digital Public Goods (DPG) Explorer is a platform built to showcase open-source technologies addressing global challenges, powered by data from the DPG Registry API. I developed it to help users discover, evaluate, and engage with digital public goods aligned with the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals.

View Project
Tools: PowerBI, Python
EvidenceBase.ai

EvidenceBase.ai is an LLM-powered internal knowledge base designed to help businesses unlock the value of their internal content. It combines advanced natural language processing techniques, including named entity recognition, text classification, and generative AI, to enable intuitive querying and retrieval of key information. This tool transforms how organizations access and leverage their knowledge, making insights more actionable and accessible.

Tools: Python, Streamlit, Spacy, Postgres/PGVector
